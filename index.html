In all honesty, I define life as a game that I play and choose the choices that build up my character. Ever since I was born, all I have wanted is to provide for my parents, make them proud, and help people who are in need. Lessons I've learned is that you can't always please everyone. Some people just can’t understand you, regardless of how hard I do something to please them.

Most of my achievements are being able to be a top honors consecutively throughout elementary and high school. I became an amateur at certain talents such as illustration, teaching, coding, and writing, it's a huge achievement for me that I also won awards along with my talents.

Tributes to the People I Know

I give tribute to my beloved family and my dog. My very close friends and all the mentors I’ve met and learned so much from. They helped me become the person I am today and I couldn’t be as grateful and give them the biggest tribute in shaping myself.

Family Background and Culture

I have two younger brothers and a mother and father. I grew up in a very open family, where I can give my thoughts and tell what happens to me regardless if it’s trivial. My family is very supportive of my future and I am taking advantage of all the lessons I’ve learned to push myself so that one day I can give back to them. The culture within my home is the typical Filipino family culture, yet being blooded into Japanese culture from my grandmother’s side, I also adopted certain cultures and values from them.
